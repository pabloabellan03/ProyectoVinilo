About Us

Vinyl Record Store specializes in collectible records from the 60s to the 90s. We offer a wide variety of rare Spanish and British records, as well as a custom search system for albums. We also carry music books, comics, and movie covers. Our mission is to recover and preserve forgotten albums, treating them as a valuable part of audiovisual culture.
